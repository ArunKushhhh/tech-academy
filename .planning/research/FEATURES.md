# Project Research: Features

## Environment
- **Domain:** Static Website for Educational Institute
- **Context:** Subsequent milestone (Adding catalog, info, and contact form to existing skeleton)

## Capabilities

### Table Stakes
- **Clear Value Proposition (Hero Section):** Who you are, who it's for, and the unique hook.
- **Course Catalog:** Clear list or grid of available courses/programs.
- **Academy Info/Philosophy:** About Us, Mission, Values.
- **Contact Form:** High-conversion form (Name, Email, Phone, Interesting Course, Message) directed to the educator.
- **Testimonials/Social Proof:** Reviews from past students or parents.
- **Mobile Responsiveness:** Must look perfect on mobile since parents/students primarily browse on phones.

### Differentiators
- **Micro-interactions & Premium UI:** Using GSAP/Motion to make the site feel elite and modern (already started).
- **Sticky Call-To-Action (CTA):** A button that follows the user as they scroll, constantly nudging them to the Contact Form.
- **Interactive Course Filters:** Ability to filter courses by age group or topic.

### Anti-features (Do NOT build)
- **User Accounts / Profiles:** Not a student portal.
- **Payment Processing (Stripe, etc.):** Funnel goes to the educator first.
- **Complex Blogs/News:** Keep the scope focused on the landing page and conversion.
